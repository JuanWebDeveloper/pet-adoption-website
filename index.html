About Us

We are an animal protection organization specialized, since 2021, in helping abused and / or abandoned dogs and cats; a totally independent Colombian organization that does not receive subsidies from official organizations, companies or political parties.
Dues from our members and donations from our supporters make our work sustainable and preserve our freedom of action.
We have a veterinary center in Medellin open to the public, which allocates its income entirely to the care of abandoned animals, and a reception center in Aranjuez.
Direct action encompasses both rescues of abused and neglected animals, as well as taking legal action against the perpetrators of crimes.

Our services

sterilization of dogs

When rescuing a dog, we take care of sterilizing it in order to prevent uterine infections and breast cancer, which is fatal in about 50 percent of dogs.

dog vaccination

All the dogs that are ready to be adopted are vaccinated and dewormed, the dogs that we put for adoption is because they are in excellent health and comply with all their vaccinations.

dog training

As you know, dogs in the streets tend to have aggressive behaviors or simply do not trust humans, that is why before a dog can be adopted we train and teach it to trust humans this so that when it is adopted it can fit into your new life in a simpler way.




Requirements to adopt

optimal home
Economic stability
Quality contract

A home in good condition

It is essential that you have an optimal space for the pet, that is, that your home has enough space, keeps it clean and is a stable place for the animal to feel liked and does not generate stress, a small place can generate stress in the dog And therefore it can cause the pet to get sick, at the time of making the adoption request an advisor will visit your home to verify that it is a valid place to have a pet.

Economic stability

When adopting one of our pets, it must be verified that the person has a stable job or an economic situation good enough to meet all the expenses that have a dog entails, that is why when adopting a dog request a labor certificate with which it can be verified that you are currently working and have a fixed income.

quality contract signing

If you meet all the requirements to adopt, you must sign a contract in which you agree to care for and ensure the welfare of the animal, you agree to things such as ensuring that it receives food, take it once a month to the veterinarian, vaccinate it when necessary, among others. things, we do this contract with the sole purpose that pets are as good as possible in their homes, one of our accessors may visit you at any time to verify that everything is being fulfilled, if for any reason the person is breaching the contract The pet will be removed immediately and a fine will be charged depending on how bad the animal is.
